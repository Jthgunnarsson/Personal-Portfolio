Soup Robot

Requirements

The requirements that the robot had to meet were that it had to be able to feet the professor with a spoon, it had to be able to sense if the soup was to hot and see what kind of soup it was.

The robot was designed in Solidworks and manufactured out of acrylic plastic with a lazercutter and out of pla plastic with a 3D Printer.

The robot has two steppermotors and one servo motor which are controlled with an Arduino Uno and a motordriver. It also has an light sensor which was used to determine what kind of soup it was and an heat sensor to find the tempeture.
