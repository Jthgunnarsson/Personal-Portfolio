Prototyping a Prostetic Hand

The group consisted of me and Tómas.

The hand is a wireless robotic hand which is controlled using a button in the shoe.

It was designed in Solidworks and manufactured with a 3D-printer using PLA and TPU Plastic. The fingers are made out of TPU plastic which is rubber like thus the fingers are able to bend and we are able to control the how much the finger bends and the stiffness of them.

There is one big servo motor which operates all fingers and thumb. It is able to do this because there is a fishing line that is attached to the tip of the fingers and thumb and to the motor. Thus able to control the fingers much like a puppet on strings.

The hand is able to lift simple things like a mug or hold on a door knob but because the motor pulls on all fingers at ones it is not able to do dexterous movements.

But it is able to hold on a soda can while it is being opened :).
